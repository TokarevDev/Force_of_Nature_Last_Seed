Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Last Seed: Survival (“the Game”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the Game.

The Game is developed and published by Oleksandr Tokarev.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account, log in, provide a name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information to play the Game.

The Game may store basic gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as settings, progress, preferences, and game state. This information is used only to provide core game functionality and improve the gameplay experience.

The Game may use third-party advertising services, such as Unity Ads, to display rewarded ads, interstitial ads, or other advertising content.

Third-party advertising services may collect and process certain data, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, app interaction data, diagnostics, and other data necessary to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

These services may use collected data for purposes such as ad delivery, fraud prevention, analytics, performance measurement, and service improvement.

You can learn more about Unity’s privacy practices here:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. Analytics

The current version of the Game may use technical diagnostics or analytics tools to understand crashes, performance issues, and general gameplay behavior. If analytics services are used, the collected data is used to improve stability, performance, balance, and user experience.

We do not use analytics data to personally identify individual users.

4. How We Use Information

Information handled by the Game or third-party services may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining game functionality;
  • Saving local game progress and preferences;
  • Displaying advertisements, if ads are enabled in the Game;
  • Measuring ad performance and preventing fraud;
  • Improving gameplay balance, stability, and performance;
  • Detecting and fixing bugs or crashes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Data may be processed by third-party service providers, such as advertising or analytics providers, only as necessary to provide their services inside the Game.

Third-party providers process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us so that we can review and, if necessary, delete the information.

7. Data Retention

Local gameplay data may remain on your device until you delete the Game or clear its local data.

Data processed by third-party services is retained according to the privacy policies and retention practices of those third-party providers.

8. Your Choices

You may be able to limit ad tracking through your device settings.

On Android, you can manage advertising personalization and reset your advertising ID through your Google settings, depending on your device and Android version.

You can also delete local game data by uninstalling the Game or clearing the app data in your device settings.

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Game. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
